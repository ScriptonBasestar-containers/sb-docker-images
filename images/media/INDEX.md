## Why Self-hosted Media?

- **Ownership**: Your media, your server
- **Privacy**: No tracking or recommendations based on listening habits
- **No subscriptions**: One-time setup, no monthly fees
- **Full library**: Play any format, no licensing restrictions
- **Offline access**: Your server, your network
