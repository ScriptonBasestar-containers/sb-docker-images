## Why Self-hosted Analytics?

- **Privacy**: Data stays on your servers
- **GDPR Compliance**: Full control over user data
- **No sampling**: Access to raw data
- **Cost**: No per-pageview pricing
- **Customization**: Modify tracking as needed
